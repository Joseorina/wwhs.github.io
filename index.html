Our Story

Introducing Webuye West Hoticultural Society

On 17th July 2023, our coperative society was formed and formally registerd. Webuye West farmers have been dependent on maize and sugarcane farming. However these enterprises have not fully benefited the farmes because maize and sugarcane require large farms to be economicallly viable but our land sizes are declining with time hence the need for horticulture farming which does require huge chunks fo lands. Therefore a few farmers have felt there was need to sensitize and bring together more farmers into hoticulture.

Governance and Management

The Society is managed by nine Management Committee members and three supervisory committee members are soon to appointed.

Vision

To make Webuye West Sub-County a leading hoticulture producer in Bungoma County and beyond.

Mission

To promote environmentally friendly high quality and competititve production of highhoticultural produce.

News

Our Avocados.

Grown in the sun-drenched orchards of Webuye, our avocados benefit from the perfect blend of fertile soil, optimal climate, and expert care. From creamy Hass avocados to vibrant green varieties, each fruit is nurtured to perfection, handpicked at the peak of ripeness, and delivered fresh to your doorstep.

Sustainability.

As stewards of the land, we recognize the importance of sustainable farming practices. From water conservation and soil health to biodiversity and reducing carbon footprint, we are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact while maximizing the long-term health of our orchards.

Governance & Management.

The Society is managed by nine Management Committee members and three supervisory committee members are soon to appointed.

Community Support.

We believe in giving back to the communities that support us. Through initiatives like educational programs, community events, and partnerships with local organizations, we strive to enrich the lives of those around us and foster a spirit of cooperation and goodwill.

Quality Assurance.
Quality is at the heart of everything we do. Our rigorous quality assurance processes ensure that only the finest avocados make it from our orchards to your table. From cultivation to packaging, every step is meticulously monitored to maintain the highest standards of freshness, flavor, and nutritional value.

Membership.

The Society has 24 fully registered members but there are over 50 who have shown interest. We aim to have enlisted 3000 members by the year 2027. Membership subscription is Kshs 500, while minimum shareholding is Kshs 5,000. So far 9 members are fully paid shareholders.
